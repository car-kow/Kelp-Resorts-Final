ABOUT US

Welcome to Kelp Resorts

Located within the tropical wonders of the Caribbean, Kelp Resorts offers numerous sights for both the relaxed vacationer and the energetic adventurer. The resort itself is stationed on Azure Island, overseen by the Lighthouse on Starry Island. Azure Island is home to plenty of beaches and tropical jungles. Our many amenities will ensure a comfortable stay, while our unique ecology will be sure to dazzle and amaze guests. One thing is for sure; you won’t believe it until you see it.

Our History

Rediscovered in 1907 by our founder, Sir Dennis Walton, the island seemingly offered little of distinguishing value compared to its surrounding territory. Having been in search of undiscovered treasure after a long series of fruitless expeditions, he was dismayed. However, Walton quickly found his fortunes turned around upon discovering that the long-uninhabited island’s shores were the source of Super Kelp, whose leafy green material proved to be an excellent and highly sustainable biofuel whose efficiency superseded even coal. It’s also a delicious yet healthy snack that you are welcome to try at any of our renowned restaurants. Exploring further into the jungles, he also was elated to discover numerous peculiar and unique creatures that defy scientific explanation.

An Eco-Friendly Resort

Kelp Resorts is proud to have its operations run entirely through our sustainable and ecologically friendly Super Kelp. We are also proud to have our creature enclosures be ranked among the most accommodating in the world. Rest easy and explore playfully across both of our islands as you learn to combine relaxation and adventure into one singular yet unforgettable experience. There’s plenty to discover and always more time to kick back and enjoy the sun-soaked coasts at Kelp Resorts.
